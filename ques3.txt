Medium 1: Arranging Coins

Prompt:
You have n coins and you want to build a staircase . 
The staircase consists of k rows where the ith row 
has exactly i coins. Return the number of complete 
rows of the staircase you will build.

Sample Input 1:
5

Sample Output 1:
2

Sample Input 2:
8

Sample Output 2:
3
